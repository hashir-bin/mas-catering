About Mas Caterers

We are overwhelmed to introduce our venture as one of the first and finest catering service provider in Mangalore, karnataka India.
............

MAS was established in 1964 by Mr. Ahmed. Initially started trades in wooden crates for exports. Later in 1968 diversified into hiring Furniture, Shamiyana, and Cooking Utensils . In the year 1994 MAS further expanded into Catering Service & now spread across cities of Karnataka & Kerala.
